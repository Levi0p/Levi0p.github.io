Mixture-of-Experts Layer for Named Entity Recognition

Python, PyTorch, HuggingFace

  • Implemented a Mixture-of-Experts layer from scratch (4 expert MLPs, top-2 gating, 4.2M params vs. 3.7M baseline) and integrated it into a BiLSTM tagger for named-entity recognition.
  • Designed the MoE block with differentiable top-2 routing and a residual + LayerNorm connection to prevent representation collapse.
  • Ran ablation studies on CoNLL-2003: entity-F1 improved from 71.90% to 72.89%, while accuracy dipped slightly by 0.32%.

LoRA-Fine-Tuned, RAG-Augmented LLM for Domain-Specific QA

Python, PyTorch, HuggingFace, FAISS

  • Fine-tuned LLaMA with LoRA (parameter-efficient fine-tuning) on a curated domain-specific instruction dataset.
  • Added a FAISS retrieval-augmented pipeline over the domain corpus to reduce hallucination and improve factual grounding.
  • Evaluated with BLEU and ROUGE; LoRA + RAG outperformed zero-shot prompting on answer relevance and factual accuracy.
